Mission goal
============

The mischievous imp met a fairy, and they are having a friendly 
contest to compare their magic.
They met in the cellar, and imp is conjuring lumps of coal while the 
fairy is conjuring delicate snowflakes.

You need to remove the imp's spells and the coal that litters the 
cellar. Don't touch the snowflakes!


Remark
------

Do not kill the imp or the fairy.


Useful commands
================

pstree PID
  Print the list of processes with their parent / child 
  relationship.

  If no PID is given, show the list of all processes with their 
  parent / child relationship.

  Useful options:
    -p   show the PID of processes
    $$   This variable contains the PID of the current shell and can
         be given as the PID.

kill N
  Send the termination signal to process number N.
  Remark: N is called PID, or "process identifier".
