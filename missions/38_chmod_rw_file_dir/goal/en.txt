Mission goal
============

The safe in the throne room contains the king's crown.
You need steal it and store it in your chest.

Also, the base of the crown is inscribed with a magical sequence of 
digits. Look at those digits and remember them.


Useful commands
===============

ls -l
  Lists the files with their important meta-data.

  Access permissions are listed first on each line.

chmod [OPTIONS] FILE
  Modifies the permissions for eth file (or directory).

  Refer to the manual to discover which options are available.
