Mission goal
============

While wandering around the first floor of the dungeon, I stopped in 
front of some magnificent paintings. I am myself fond of fine art.
Add a copy (same name, same content) of the oldest painting to your 
chest.


Secondary objectives
--------------------

Take a moment to admire the sheer beauty of the paintings.


Useful commands
===============

ls -l
  print the list of files, with many additional information, 
  including last modification date

cat FILE
  display the contents of the file
