Mission goal
============

While wandering around the first floor of the main tower, some 
magnificent paintings catch your eye. Add a copy (same name and same 
content) of the oldest painting to your chest.


Secondary objectives
--------------------

Take a moment to admire the sheer beauty of the paintings.


Useful commands
===============

ls -l
  Print the list of files of the current directory, with additional 
  information including last modification date.

cat FILE
  Display the contents of the file.
