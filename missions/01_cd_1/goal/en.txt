Mission goal
============

Go to the top of the main tower of the castle.


Useful commands
===============

cd LOCATION
  Move to the given LOCATION.
  Remark: ``cd`` stands for "change directory".

pwd
  See the path to your current location.
  Remark: ``pwd`` stands for "print working directory".

ls
  See a list of locations that are currently accessible.
  Remark: ``ls`` is an abbreviation of "list".

gash reset
  Restart the mission from the beginning.
