Mission goal
============

Go to the top of the dungeon.


Useful commands
===============

cd LOCATION
  Lets you move to the given LOCATION.
  Remark: ``cd`` stands for “change directory”.

pwd
  Shows the current location.
  Remark: ``pwd`` stands for “print working directory”.

ls
  Lists the locations that are currently accessible.

gash reset
  Lets you restart the mission from the beginning.
