Mission goal
============

The old man seems to enjoy your company very much. He invites 
you to stay for supper, and starts preparing a delicious stew 
for the both of you. While getting the cauldron ready he asks 
for your help. Read the steps of the recipe from his book.

In order to validate the mission, your last command prior to 
``gash check`` must show the steps of the recipe, but not its 
title nor any blank lines.


Useful commands
===============

cat FILE
  Display the contents of the FILE.

tail FILE
  Print the last 10 lines of the FILE.

tail -n K FILE
  Print the last K lines of the FILE.
