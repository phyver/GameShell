Mission goal
============

The old man seems to enjoy your company very much. He invites you to 
stay for supper, and starts preparing a delicious stew for the both 
of you. While getting the cauldron ready he asks for your help.
Read him the steps of the recipe from his book.

In order to validate the mission, your last command prior to ``gsh 
check`` must show the steps of the recipe, but not its title.


Useful commands
===============

cat FILE
  Display the contents of the file.

tail FILE
  Print the last 10 lines of the file.

tail -n K FILE
  Print the last K lines of the file.
